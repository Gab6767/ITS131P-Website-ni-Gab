About us

   Navigating expansive malls in the Philippines often leaves visitors vulnerable to losing their personal belongings, quickly transforming a good day into a stressful ordeal. Retracing steps manually causes significant time disruption, confusion, and panic for owners who are often left unsure of how to retrieve their items. At the same time, managing these misplaced goods becomes an operational burden for item finders, security guards, and customer service staff handling manual tracking systems.


   To bridge this gap, our database-driven, web-based management system automates and streamlines the entire reporting and tracking process. By replacing outdated manual logging with an efficient, real-time digital interface, we provide seamless time convenience, mitigate anxiety, and drastically enhance item recovery efficiency for everyone involved.

Why this exist

   Navigating expansive, complex malls in the Philippines, particularly One Ayala, often leaves individuals vulnerable to losing their personal belongings. We recognized that without a proper system, this leads to time disruption, confusion, and panic. Specifically, we identified several major pain points:

  • Time Disruption: Retracing steps to find lost items can take a great deal of time, including multiple floors and sections.
  • Confusion and Panic: Individuals may not know how to report or retrieve their lost items, leading to increased stress and panic.
  • Burden on Staff: Handling lost items often becomes a burden for customer service, security guards, or other bystander employees who may not have an efficient system in place.

Our solution

   To address these challenges, we created TraceOneAyala, a web-based interface accessible from any browser, including Safari, Google, and Edge, with no software installation required. Our solution is connected to a MySQL database specifically structured for the One Ayala branch to manage daily operations securely.


   Our system provides the following features to ensure a seamless experience:

  • Reports & Analytics: The system can generate real-time reports to support data-driven decisions.
  • Data Integrity: The database ensures accurate, consistent, and secure data at all times.
  • Search & Filter: Users can quickly locate records using search and filter functionalities.
